Random Tips
■ Swing club UNDER ‘water-noodle’ so <> over-the-top (swing outside) *
● > clubhead BACK/behind/under 'plane' coming down
■ avoid contacting ball on inside of club face
■ Body > fwd a bit earlier (not AFTER hit ball)
■ Don’t hit ‘low on club face’ (thin) - lowest 2-3 grooves
 ∆ Stand closer to ball *
 ∆ Relax muscles - AFTER BKSWG (gravity)
■ Brush grass in front of ball
● shoulders/arms/etc loose/FLOPPY > club-face lower @ impact
■ Feet wider apart > contact higher on face
● Wide stance ++ (so <> ?sway)
■ Inside path > "Throw to R field" *
■ Wrist rotation > close face so <> slice
■ Cocking wrists > staightens L arm
■ CLOSER & TALLER": stand closer to ball & more upright (so <> bob)
■ easy to 'roll club face over early' if body stalls out
- if hips & torso start 'unwinding sooner' can 'time the club face' (later rollover) so it's easier to get it to start out on target (rather than to L) ['club face awareness']
■ delay wrist impact snap/rotation/'rollover' until hands well past ball (NB!)  *
■ CHEST turn w/ hips (doesn't stay back w/ head)
■ LOOP > inside-out
■ Natural Rhythm/Tempo - less effort but can be slow or fast (> ^ distance)
■ Chest before arms *
● if concentrate on inside-out TOO MUCH <> chest leading arms
